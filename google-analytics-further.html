Course Overview:

The ultimate next step in refining your Google Analytics expertise.

This course will go beyond the surface and take your existing knowledge and hone and sharpen it, allowing you to dig up much more valuable insights to better predict your audience and enhance your attraction and retention activities.

This course will cover:

  • segments
  • annotations
  • more complex goals and conversions
  • real time analytics
  • linking with Google Adwords
  • setting up and using custom dashboards
  • targeted campaign tracking using UTM codes
  • filters, keyword tracking, campaign tracking
  • syncing with Google Search Console

We’ll also delve deeper in to user behaviour and look at the secondary dimensions of page data and you’ll come away with a clearer picture of where to best spend your time and focus.

Understand – and we mean REALLY understand – your online marketing because understanding is the first step to improvement.

Course objectives:

  • further develop knowledge of the capabilities of Google Analytics
  • further develop confidence with using some of the more advanced features of Google Analytics
  • develop the skills to set up goal and event tracking, to monitor conversions

This course is for:

  • savvy business owners and managers
  • agency, in house or independent marketers responsible for driving traffic to and/or activity on websites
  • bloggers
  • freelancers and consultants
  • web developers who want to a greater understanding of what Google Analytics can do
  • anyone who wants to improve their use of Google Analytics
  • past participants of Introduction to Google Analytics.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • modern computer training room
  • individual computers (PC) and the necessary internet access and software
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

  • a business or personal website or blog with Google Analytics installed
  • a minimum of one month’s complete data collected
  • a basic understanding of how to use and interpret Google Analytics’ default reports
  • Introduction to Google Analytics course (recommended)

Each session is restricted to limited participants (generally six to twenty attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
