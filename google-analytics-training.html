Google Analytics is a convenient, free tool to help you analyse exactly who is visiting your website. It has a number of useful capabilities that provide you with multiple sets of information that you can use to your advantage by improving your website, understanding your consumers’ behaviours and collecting their demographic profile information.

There are many incredible things that can be done with Google Analytics and we’re dedicated to showing you exactly what they are and how to make the most of it.

Our courses are not for the faint hearted though; they are practical and in depth learning systems that are targeted towards marketing professionals, business owners, site developers and senior managers.
