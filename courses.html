With an emphasis on results and learning, we break down complex digital marketing subjects so you have a thorough understanding and are able to apply what is relevant in your own business, workplace or website.

No longer do you have to feel intimidated by or fumble your way through digital marketing facets and these powerful digital tools. Get to know how they work and how to best apply tools, techniques and tips to your current role, company or strategy.

These training courses are ideal for:

  • micro, small and medium business owners, managers and marketing/communications professionals
  • agency account, marketing, digital and social media managers
  • website owners, content writers and editors
  • bloggers
  • freelancers and consultants
  • web developers and designers.

And anyone who wants to:

  • understand the capabilities of behind the scenes and front of line digital marketing
  • stop being overwhelmed by Google tools when reporting, optimising and marketing
  • jump into the world of digital marketing but are not sure where to start
  • improve their search engine rankings
  • provide comprehensive reporting on a site they manage
  • learn in a supportive environment
  • upskill and refresh their existing Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, social media marketing, email marketing, ecommerce optimisation and SEO knowledge.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (generally six to twenty attendees), so you will fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice. Scout Digital Training works with some of the most experienced and renowned professionals in Australia.

Not only will you be provided with thorough training but you will come away with a stack of resources including reporting and content templates, a work booklet of best practices and key takeaway information and training notes, ensuring that you will be able to action what you’ve learnt during any of sessions.

This training will not just be another professional development session, all the courses have tangible outcomes that will help you get real results.
