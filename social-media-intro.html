Workshop overview:

This real world overview workshop, with the beginner in mind, is an introduction to the world of social media and best practice use for business and will set you on the right path to effective social media marketing.

It will succinctly provide an overview of how to make best use of social media platforms for businesses and organisations to meet your objectives and strategic goals. It will also take a comprehensive look at the major players in social (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and Twitter) and how to best set up them up, optimise and begin to build an audience.

Throughout the duration of the workshop, we will look at the key demographics and forecasted trends within the Australian social media landscape and how these affect your organisation’s social media presence.

This workshop will cover:

  • Australian social media user numbers, insights and demographics
  • social media channel and setting up best practices
  • Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn newsfeeds
  • understanding the Facebook algorithm
  • how to publish content on these main social media channels
  • how to genuinely build engagement
  • community management fundamentals.

Workshop objectives:

  • to provide an overview of how to make best use of social media marketing for businesses and organisations
  • to develop confidence in using social media effectively.

This workshop is for:

  • beginners with no assumed knowledge
  • SME business owners and managers
  • government departments
  • marketing graduates wanting to gain practical knowledge and skills
  • marketing coordinators with no social media experience
  • marketing professionals wanting to gain current knowledge and skills.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

  • None, although admin/editor access to existing business social media channels (i.e. Facebook Page, not personal profile) to work with is ideal (but not essential).

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
