Workshop overview:

You already understand the power of social media, so take it further with a strategic approach to planning, setting up and managing effective Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns for multiple accounts.

This workshop is designed specifically for social media marketers, consultants and agencies who work with multiple clients and accounts.

It is essential that social media advertising campaigns are both effective AND efficient, and this workshop will show you how.

This workshop will cover:

  • the power of paid advertising and the significant results it can bring
  • strategic campaign planning
  • a deep dive into Facebook Ads Manager
  • how to interpret and utilise social media advertising insights.

Workshop objectives:

  • to strategically plan ad campaigns to meet business objectives
  • to develop understanding and confidence in all options available for advertising on Facebook and Instagram

This workshop is for:

  • people who manage multiple Facebook Pages and/or Instagram accounts
  • if you manage an individual Facebook Page/Instagram account, please see our Mastering Facebook and Instagram Ads (for individual accounts) workshop

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

  • Admin access to multiple Facebook Pages/Instagram accounts
  • an understanding of social media and its application in a business environment (this is covered in our Social Media for Business workshop)
  • access to Facebook Ads Manager
  • access to and an understanding of Facebook Business Manager (this is covered in our Mastering Facebook Business Manager workshop)
  • please download the free Facebook Ads Manager app to your smartphone prior to the session (for managing and monitoring ads via your mobile device).
  • Facebook Ads Manager app for iOS
  • Facebook Ads Manager app for Android

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
