Workshop overview:

LinkedIn is THE social media network for professional online networking. It has evolved from being an online resume to a powerful place to network and market yourself, or your organization.

This workshop is presented in two parts:

  • LinkedIn company pages
  • LinkedIn personal profiles

Attend either or both, for a holistic understanding of how to make the most of this channel.

LinkedIn company pages will explore:

  • setting up a LinkedIn company page and page settings
  • attracting page followers
  • publishing updates
  • engagement
  • company page analytics

LinkedIn personal profiles will explore:

  • setting up a LinkedIn personal profile and profile settings
  • the difference between free and Premium accounts
  • profile sections
  • publishing updates
  • making and managing connections
  • engagement

Please note:

  • both sections focus on the free options available for company pages and personal profiles. We will not look at the Careers section for pages, or other paid tools
  • we do not explore LinkedIn advertising in this workshop. This is covered in our Mastering LinkedIn and Twitter Advertising workshop.

Workshop objectives:

  • to understand the best practices for using LinkedIn for individuals (personal profile)
  • to understand the best practices for using LinkedIn for businesses and organisations (company pages)
  • to understand the various options available for managing, sharing content and engaging with your audience
  • to develop the confidence and skills to competently manage a personal profile and/or a company page.

This workshop is for:

  • individuals who want to understand and make best use of their personal LinkedIn profile
  • those responsible for managing LinkedIn company pages to provide their organization with a profile on LinkedIn.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

Either:

  • a new LinkedIn personal profile (or one that hasn’t been used for some time), or
  • plans to set up a new LinkedIn personal profile.

AND/OR

  • a new LinkedIn company page (or one that hasn’t been used for some time), or
  • plans to set up a new LinkedIn company page (requires a LinkedIn personal profile).

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
