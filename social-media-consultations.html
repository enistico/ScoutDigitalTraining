Social media marketing is evolving quickly, and it is hard to know what you need, or where to spend your energy.

In these limited sessions we are offering a unique opportunity for a one-on-one session with our social media scout, Jen Evison, to review your social media channels and situation.

For 1.5 hours you can discuss your situation and requirements with Jen, and she will provide as much insight and support as she can, along with any recommendations for further tools or training.

Bring along all log in details and any existing resources, and let one of Adelaide’s leading social media marketers help you.

Further training sessions are not essential, however should you book a social media training workshop within 6 months of consultation, we will discount the full value of the consultation off the training price (must be redeemed by the same person).

Don’t miss this opportunity for dedicated time with a skilled social media professional!
