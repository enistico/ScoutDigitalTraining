Workshop overview:

Each social media channel has its own tone, etiquette and nuances, and Twitter is no exception. This workshop will clearly explain how to understand and use Twitter to raise your personal or professional brand.

We will explore account set up and profile settings, publishing updates (tweets), engaging with others, and growing your community.

We will also examine tools to help manage Twitter and review Twitter Analytics.

This workshop will cover:

  • account set up and profile settings
  • publishing tweets
  • different types of tweets
  • using hashtags
  • retweets, @replies, @mentions and more
  • making and using lists
  • tools to help manage your Twitter account
  • an introduction to Twitter analytics.

Workshop objectives:

  • to understand the best practices for setting up a Twitter account, for personal or business use
  • to understand the various options available for managing, sharing content and engaging with your audience using Twitter
  • to develop the confidence and skills to competently manage a Twitter account.

This workshop is for:

  • business owner/operators responsible for managing their Twitter account
  • administration or marketing staff responsible for managing an organisation’s Twitter account
  • marketers who want to understand best practices for managing a Twitter account
  • freelancers and consultants who want to understand best practices for managing a Twitter account
  • individuals who want to make best use of Twitter as part of their their personal or professional use.

What’s included:

  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

Either:

  • a new Twitter account (or one that hasn’t been used for some time), or
  • plans to set up a new Twitter account (requires an email address, that isn’t in use with another Twitter account)

and a smartphone.

Please note:

  • you are required to bring your own laptop.
  • we do not explore Twitter advertising in this workshop. This is covered in our Mastering LinkedIn and Twitter Advertising workshop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
