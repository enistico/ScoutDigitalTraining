Workshop overview:

Advertising via your social media platforms is one of the most cost effective and potent ways to reach your exact audience these days and it’s set to explode even more over the next few years.

With billions of dollars each year (and rising significantly) being invested, you can be sure that your competitors are in the game, even if you aren’t. But we understand that you want to get it right, from the start, otherwise you are wasting time and money.

You will be introduced to Facebook Ads Manager platform and the most commonly used paid elements for Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns to complement your organic content updates.

This workshop will cover:

  • the power of paid advertising and the significant results it can bring
  • an introduction to Facebook Ads Manager
  • setting up Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns
  • an introduction to social media advertising insights.

Workshop objectives:

  • to develop understanding and confidence in the options available for advertising on Facebook and Instagram
  • how to use social media advertising to complement day-to-day “organic” updates

This workshop is for:

  • people who manage an individual Facebook Page and/or Instagram account
  • if you manage multiple Facebook Pages/Instagram accounts, please see our Mastering Facebook and Instagram Ads (for multiple accounts) workshop

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

  • a Facebook Business Page and/or Instagram Business account
  • an understanding of social media and its application in a business environment (this is covered in our Social Media for Business workshop)
  • access to Facebook Ads Manager and a campaign/ad that you would like to create and set live. We can provide assistance with this if required. You will be asked during the online registration process.
  • please download the free Facebook Ads Manager app to your smartphone prior to the session (for managing and monitoring ads via your mobile device).
  • Facebook Ads Manager app for iOS
  • Facebook Ads Manager app for Android

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (three to six attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
