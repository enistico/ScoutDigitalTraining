Workshop overview:

Instagram is growing exponentially as not only a popular social media platform, but an effective place to market businesses and organisations.

This workshop will explain all of the options available for making best use of your Instagram account to reach and engage your audience.

This workshop will cover:

  • setting up an Instagram account
  • personal versus Business Instagram accounts
  • reviewing how to create and publish Instagram content
  • reviewing Instagram Stories
  • creating content on the go for Instagram
  • photography tips
  • reporting using Instagram Insights.

Workshop objectives:

  • to understand the best practices for managing an Instagram account for an organization or business
  • to understand the various options available for managing, sharing content and engaging with your audience using Instagram
  • to develop the confidence and skills to competently manage an Instagram business account.

This workshop is for:

  • business owner/operators responsible for managing their Instagram account
  • administration or marketing staff responsible for managing an organisation’s Instagram account
  • marketers who want to understand best practices for managing an Instagram business account.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

Either:

  • a new Instagram account (or one that hasn’t been used for some time), or
  • plans to set up a new Instagram account

and a smartphone.

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
