Workshop overview:

Facebook remains an incredibly powerful platform for connecting people and organisations, however it is becoming increasingly challenging to reach and engage Page’s audiences in the Facebook Newsfeed.

There is a wealth of options available to Page Admins to reach and engage their audience, this workshop will ensure you are armed with the information and skills to effectively market your organization using a Facebook Page.

This workshop will cover:

  • setting up a Facebook Page
  • reviewing all aspects of a standard Facebook Page
  • understanding Facebook Page roles
  • reviewing Facebook Page settings
  • default and custom Page tabs
  • recommended image sizes
  • different types of Facebook posts
  • best practices for Facebook posts
  • publishing, scheduling and draft posts
  • best practices for managing engagement
  • interpreting your success through Page Insights.

Workshop objectives:

  • to understand the best practices for setting up a Facebook Page for an organization or business
  • to understand the various options available for managing, sharing content and engaging with your audience using a Facebook Page
  • to develop the confidence and skills to competently manage a Facebook Page.

This workshop is for:

  • business owner/operators responsible for managing their Facebook Page
  • administration or marketing staff responsible for managing an organisation’s Facebook Page
  • marketers who want to understand best practices for managing a Facebook Page
  • freelancers and consultants who want to understand best practices for managing a Facebook Page.

What’s included:

  • light refreshments
  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

Either:

  • a new Facebook Page (or one that hasn’t been used for some time), or
  • plans to set up a new Facebook Page (personal Facebook profile is required).

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.

Be prepared to make the most of your Facebook Page!
