Workshop overview:

Twitter and LinkedIn hold a wealth of data that you can strategically leverage to grow your online communities, and reach and engage your audience.

This workshop will explain the advertising and targeting options available within LinkedIn and Twitter to enable you to plan, set up and track your campaigns and results.

This workshop will cover:

  • the power of paid advertising and the significant results it can bring
  • Twitter and LinkedIn advertising options
  • an introduction to social media advertising insights.

Workshop objectives:

  • to develop understanding and confidence in the options available for advertising on Twitter and LinkedIn
  • how to use social media advertising to achieve business goals

This workshop is for:

  • people who manage Twitter accounts and/or LinkedIn company pages, and want to understand and master their advertising options

What’s included:

  • full set of training resources
  • high quality training

Prerequisites:

  • a Twitter account and/or Admin access to a LinkedIn company page
  • an understanding of social media and its application in a business environment (this is covered in our Social Media for Business workshop)

Please note: You are required to bring your own laptop.

Each session is restricted to limited participants (six to ten attendees), so you have the opportunity to fully benefit from the trainer’s knowledge and advice.
